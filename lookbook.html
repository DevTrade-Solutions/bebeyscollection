Stylist Tips & Inspiration

Expert advice on styling African luxury pieces

Layering Traditional Prints

Mix different Ankara prints by keeping one color consistent. Pair a bold print with a more subtle one in the same color family for a cohesive look.

Try: Ankara crop top with matching print skirt

Accessorizing Statement Pieces

When wearing heavily embroidered or beaded pieces, keep accessories minimal. Let one statement piece shine without competition.

Try: Simple gold hoops with beaded dresses

Color Coordination

African prints often feature multiple colors. Pick one dominant color from the print and match your shoes and bag to it for a polished look.

Try: Gold accessories with earth-toned prints
